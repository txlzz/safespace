About Safespace

Our Mission

Through education, support, and advocacy, the Safe Space organization works to enhance mental health for all individuals and families.

We make an effort to increase public awareness of mental health and the mind-body connection between the physical and mental quality of life

Our Vision

We want to create a mental health climate that is a model for the rest of the country. We are a community that sees mental health as a critical component of total well-being in this climate.

We recognize that mental illness, like physical sickness, is a medically curable disorder that can affect anybody, that it can be prevented, and that people who suffer from it can recover their health.

Our Values

We embody our values in the way we treat our clients, engage with all of our stakeholders, and work with and communicate with our coworkers.

Everyone, regardless of age, color, nationality, gender, socioeconomic status has the right and responsibility to reach their full potential in our society, including the right to be treated with compassion, respect, and dignity, free of stigma and discrimination.
